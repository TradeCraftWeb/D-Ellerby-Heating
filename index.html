About Dan

Your Local Heating Expert —
Not a Call Centre

When you call D Ellerby Heating, you speak to Dan. When Dan comes to your home, he listens, explains what he's doing, and doesn't leave until the job is done right.

He started D Ellerby Heating because he believed local homeowners deserved a heating engineer who treated them like a neighbour — honest about what needed doing, clear about the cost, and reliable enough that you'd happily recommend him to your family.
